ABOUT US

SkiGeek is a newly founded company, founded in 2022 by Edward Fagerström. SkiGeek is a ski gear reseller, working together with big companies such as The Faction Collective and Sweet Protection. Despite being a fairly new company, we have gained fast success and are growing at a pretty much exponential rate. The secret behind the success has been the bussiness concept: Selling gear at an affordable price. By mounting your own bindings to your skis instead of having a retailer do it for you we are able to charge you a lot less, meaning you save money. Easier for us, more money for you, it's a win-win situation! By not buying premounted binding, you also gain the freedom of choosing the binding that suits best which gives you an even better experience out on the snow.

Can I return my product and get a refund if I'm not happy?

If you're not happy with your product, we will first see if there is anything we can do to solve the problem. However, if you're still not happy, we have a 30-day refund policy during which you can return your product and get a full refund.

Can I switch my product for another product?

Within 30-days of your initial purchase, you are eligible for a full refund, however you can also choose to switch your product for another product with a matching price. You can switch your product as long as the initial purchase was not made more than 365 days ago.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, by choosing FedEx or UPS delivery we can guarantee worldwide shipping to your location.

Where are the products produced?

Since SkiGeek is a reseller, we rely on production from the companies we collaborate with. This means that the products we sell are produced in The Faction Collectives and Sweet Protections factories. The Faction Collective produces their products in Verbier, Switzerland while Sweet Protection has their factory located in Østerdalen, Norway.
