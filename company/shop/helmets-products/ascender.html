PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

The Ascender is a triple certified, low volume and highly ventilated ski touring/mountaineering helmet. Our go-to helmet for our professional mountain guides. Made to be used going both up- and downhill. The Ascender is a truly innovative and ground-breaking product using our unique hybrid variable elasticity shell and the dual density EPS. *No earpads are supplied with this helmet. We advice wearing a thin beanie/hat.
  • Revolutionary ski touring/mountaineering helmet made to be used going both up- and downhill.
  • Advanced variable elasticity hybrid shell. Hybrid: combining a hardshell- and in-mold construction to optimize the performance.
  • Dual density shock absorbing EPS
  • 108 penetration proof vents
  • Extreme low volume
  • Integrated clips for headlamp
  • Highly packable
